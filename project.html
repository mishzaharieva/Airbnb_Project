Airbnb Data Science Project

Problem Formulation

What factors and features contribute to a successful Airbnb booking in Rio de Janeiro?

Practical Motivation

Airbnb has revolutionized travel accommodations, providing both travelers and hosts with unparalleled convenience and opportunity. However, with a multitude of listings available at varying prices, understanding the key factors that influence booking success is crucial. For potential hosts looking to maximize rental revenue, knowing which features and attributes are associated with higher booking rates can guide investment decisions. Similarly, for travelers seeking the best value while prioritizing specific amenities like 'free parking', identifying these critical factors ensures an optimal booking experience. Our goal is to uncover these pivotal elements that drive booking success in Rio de Janeiro, empowering hosts to enhance their listings and enabling travelers to make informed, value-driven choices.
